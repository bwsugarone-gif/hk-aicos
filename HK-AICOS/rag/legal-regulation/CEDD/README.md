- Landslip risk assessment required
- Proper drainage essential for slope stability

## Risk Reminder

⚠️ **All geotechnical works must be designed and supervised by Registered Geotechnical Engineer (RGE).**

⚠️ **Slope failure may cause serious casualties and property damage.**

⚠️ **Ground investigation must be adequate before design.**

⚠️ **AI Agent recommendations must be verified by qualified geotechnical professionals.**
