## Notes

- Fire services installations require FSD approval
- Registered Fire Service Installation Contractor (RFSIC) required
- FS 251 submission required
- Dangerous goods require license
- Fire safety certificate required for occupation
- Regular inspection and maintenance mandatory

---

## Risk Reminder

⚠️ **All fire services installations must be designed and installed by Registered Fire Service Installation Contractor.**

⚠️ **Fire safety systems must be maintained and tested regularly.**

⚠️ **Dangerous goods storage requires proper license and safety measures.**

⚠️ **AI Agent recommendations must be verified by qualified fire safety professionals.**
