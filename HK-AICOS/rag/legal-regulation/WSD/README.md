- All plumbing works require Licensed Plumber
- Plumbing proposal must be submitted to WSD
- Water quality testing required
- Backflow prevention devices mandatory
- Water meter installation by WSD only
- Pressure testing required before connection

## Risk Reminder

⚠️ **All plumbing works must be carried out by Licensed Plumber.**

⚠️ **Unauthorized connection to water mains is illegal.**

⚠️ **Water quality must meet WSD standards.**

⚠️ **AI Agent recommendations must be verified by Licensed Plumber.**
