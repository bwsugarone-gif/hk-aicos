## Risk Reminder

⚠️ **Fatal or serious accidents must be reported immediately to Labour Department.**

⚠️ **All construction workers must complete mandatory safety training (Green Card).**

⚠️ **Failure to comply with safety regulations may result in prosecution and site closure.**

⚠️ **AI Agent recommendations must be verified by qualified safety professionals.**
