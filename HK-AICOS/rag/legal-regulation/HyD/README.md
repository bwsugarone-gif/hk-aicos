# HyD - Highways Department
## 路政署

---

## Department Overview

**Full Name:** Highways Department (HyD)

**Chinese Name:** 路政署

**Official Website:** https://www.hyd.gov.hk/

---

## Responsibilities

- Public road and highway management
- Excavation permit for road works
- Traffic management during construction
- Road opening and reinstatement
- Slope maintenance
- Bridge and tunnel management
- Roadside utilities coordination

---

## Applicable Construction Scope

- Road opening and excavation
- Temporary traffic arrangement
- Hoarding on public road
- Utility diversion works
- Slope works adjacent to roads
- Access to construction site from public road

- Safety Agent
- PM Agent
- Legal Agent
- Foreman Agent

## Notes

- Excavation Permit (EP) required for road opening
- Temporary Traffic Arrangement (TTA) approval required
- Road reinstatement must meet HyD standards
- Traffic management plan required
- Hoarding on public road requires approval
- Utility detection before excavation mandatory
- Road opening hours may be restricted
