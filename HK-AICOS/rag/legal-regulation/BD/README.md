## Notes

- All building works require Authorized Person (AP) and/or Registered Structural Engineer (RSE)
- Building plans must be approved by Building Authority
- Consent to commence works required
- Occupation Permit required before occupation
- Verify latest amendments to Buildings Ordinance

---

## Risk Reminder

⚠️ **All structural works must be designed and supervised by Authorized Person (AP) or Registered Structural Engineer (RSE).**

⚠️ **Unauthorized building works are illegal and subject to prosecution.**
